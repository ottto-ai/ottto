# Code Of Conduct

Ottto project spaces should stay professional, respectful, and focused on
building reliable software for local agent setup, diagnostics, and telemetry
controls.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Respect different experience levels and operating environments.
- Keep technical criticism specific to code, docs, designs, or behavior.
- Handle security, privacy, and customer-impacting topics with care.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information, credentials, customer data, raw prompts, or
  raw model outputs without explicit authorization.
- Repeated disruption of issues, reviews, or discussions after maintainers have
  asked for the behavior to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block accounts;
or take other moderation actions when needed to protect project participants
and users. Report conduct concerns to support@ottto.net.
